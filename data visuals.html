Analysis

Through analyzing the data we have collected we have noticed two things.
The first thing we have noticed is that over the years poverty has decreased for all races, although hispanic people are expieriencing a rise in poverty.
The second thing we have noticed is that the poverty rate has always been highest for blacks and hispanics, and lowest for asians and whites.
This shows that people growing up in poverty are more likely to be in poverty themselves.
