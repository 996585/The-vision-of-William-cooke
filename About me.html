Favorite soccer team

Borussia Dortmund

(cco public domain) Created by suesun on https://pixabay.com/

Borussia Dortmund is a soccer team based in Dortmund, Germany that plays in the Bundesliga.
They where created in december of 1909 and where a club for the working class in Germany.
Their biggest rivals are Bayern Munich( Bayern is currently first)
They also have produced many players for the German national team like Marco Reus, Mario Gotze, and Matt Hummels.
